### Notes for whoever adapts this

- **Don't just trim — replace.** The point of starting from the full checklist is to see what categories exist, not to inherit 51 items you don't actually use.
- **Re-review this template quarterly.** Stacks change, incidents teach new lessons, and a checklist that never grows stops being useful.
- **If an item causes arguments more often than it prevents bugs, cut it or rewrite it.** A checklist that nobody trusts gets skipped entirely.
